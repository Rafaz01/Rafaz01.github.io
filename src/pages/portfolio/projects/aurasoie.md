**AuraSoie** is a premium haircare brand founded by a pharmaceutical expert with 15+ years of experience. The client needed their Shopify store redesigned and fully translated from Spanish to English to reach a broader US market.

## 🧩 What I Did

- **Theme customization** — modified the Supreme theme by Grixio to match the brand's aesthetic
- **Full translation** — translated all Liquid template files, product descriptions, and UI strings from Spanish to English
- **Contact page redesign** — rebuilt the contact page layout and copy to improve user experience
- **Performance tweaks** — optimized sections for faster load and cleaner mobile display
- **Brand consistency** — ensured typography, colors, and tone matched AuraSoie's premium positioning

## 💡 Technologies Used

- Shopify
- Liquid (Shopify templating language)
- CSS
- HTML

## 🧠 What I Learned

Working with **Liquid** taught me how templating languages work in real e-commerce environments. I learned to navigate a large existing codebase, make targeted edits without breaking other sections, and communicate changes clearly with a client who needed precise, iterative updates.
