**8th Element Podcast** is a show hosted by three long-time bhakti yogis who analyze current events and geopolitics through a Vedic wisdom lens. The name references oxygen — the 8th element — as a metaphor for life-giving, truth-seeking information.

JG Graphic was commissioned to design and develop a full WordPress website that matched the podcast's unique identity: cosmic, spiritual, and intellectually serious.

## 🧩 What I Did

- **Full WordPress build** — developed on Blocksy theme with Elementor for a fully custom layout
- **Cosmic visual design** — integrated floating planet illustrations, sacred symbols, and dark space aesthetic to match the brand
- **YouTube integration** — episode section pulls thumbnail previews directly from YouTube for each episode
- **WooCommerce store** — set up the Store section for merchandise and digital products
- **Newsletter signup** — configured email capture form for weekly truth-seeking intel
- **SEO optimization** — structured page content, meta descriptions, and headings for search visibility
- **Responsive design** — fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Multi-page structure** — Home, About Us, Contact, and Store pages

## 💡 Technologies Used

- WordPress
- Blocksy Theme
- Elementor
- WooCommerce
- CSS (custom styles)
- SEO optimization

## 🧠 What I Learned

This project pushed me to work with a **highly specific brand identity** — the cosmic, spiritual aesthetic required careful attention to typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy. I also learned to integrate **YouTube thumbnails dynamically** and configure **WooCommerce** from scratch for a content-based brand.
