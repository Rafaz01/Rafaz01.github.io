**Vista by Ilse** is a travel blog and lifestyle brand created by Ilse Martinez — a storyteller and traveler who documents her adventures across Europe and beyond. The site features travel guides, curated itineraries, a blog, and brand partnership opportunities.

JG Graphic was commissioned to design and develop the full WordPress site to match Ilse's visual identity: clean, editorial, and travel-inspired.

## 🧩 What I Did

- **Full WordPress build** — developed using WordPress and Elementor with a custom editorial layout
- **Bilingual support** — configured English/Spanish language switching for Ilse's international audience
- **Multi-page structure** — Home, About, Travel Blog, Travel Guides, Work With Me, and Helpful Links
- **Blog setup** — configured categories by destination (France, Italy, Greece, Switzerland) with featured posts
- **Newsletter integration** — set up email capture form for travel tips and adventure updates
- **Work With Me page** — built a brand partnership and collaboration page for content creator opportunities
- **SEO optimization** — structured content, meta descriptions, and headings for travel-related search terms
- **Responsive design** — fully optimized across mobile, tablet, and desktop

## 💡 Technologies Used

- WordPress
- Elementor
- CSS (custom styles)
- Bilingual plugin (EN/ES)
- SEO optimization

## 🧠 What I Learned

Working on Vista by Ilse taught me how to build for a **content creator audience** — where the design needs to stay out of the way and let the photography and writing shine. I also learned to structure a **travel blog taxonomy** (categories by destination) and set up a professional **media kit / work with me** page for brand partnerships.
