**BonitaFind** is a skincare consultation brand founded by Clare, a Colombian-American licensed esthetician based in Tampa, FL. She offers personalized virtual skincare consultations with a multicultural and bilingual approach for English and Spanish-speaking clients.

The client needed a full redesign of their existing WordPress site to better communicate their brand identity, improve the user experience, and drive more bookings through their Calendly integration.

## 🧩 What I Did

- **Full redesign** — rebuilt the site using WordPress and Elementor to match BonitaFind's feminine, professional brand aesthetic
- **Bilingual support** — configured TranslatePress for English/Spanish switching
- **Conversion-focused layout** — restructured the homepage flow to guide visitors toward booking a consultation
- **Sections redesigned** — Hero, Why Book, Meet Clare, Podcast, Testimonials, FAQ, and Newsletter
- **Calendly integration** — embedded booking flow directly into the site
- **SEO optimization** — optimized page titles, meta descriptions, and content structure
- **Responsive design** — ensured clean layout across mobile, tablet, and desktop

## 💡 Technologies Used

- WordPress
- Elementor
- CSS (custom styles)
- TranslatePress (EN/ES)
- SEO optimization

## 🧠 What I Learned

Working on BonitaFind taught me how to design for a **specific target audience** — Latina women interested in skincare — and how to translate a brand's personality into layout and visual choices. I also learned to work with **multilingual plugins** and configure them properly for a bilingual audience.
